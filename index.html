About Me

Soft interactions

I design interfaces that bring technology closer to human experience through natural communication. Using soft robotics, I create interfaces that mimic human touch with dynamic shape changes and haptic feedback. These soft interfaces allow users to interact with technology in a more expressive and fluid way, making experiences feel more human. My work focuses on making soft robotics accessible by offering tutorials and resources, helping people create their own systems using tools like 3D printers and laser cutters to experiment with dynamic, tactile communication.

I believe that by making soft robotics approachable and intuitive, we can create a future where technology seamlessly integrates into our lives, enhancing everyday experiences through natural, tactile communication.
