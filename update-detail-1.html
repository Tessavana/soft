Yuta presented his paper "Designing for Interdependence of Bees, Garden, Designer, and the Changing Season" at DIS24

This paper discusses the importance of interdependence between bees, garden, designers, and the changing season. The presentation took place at DIS24, an event dedicated to discussing design and the environment.

Read more about the paper and its implications for future design projects involving ecological systems and sustainable design practices.
